Frequently Asked Questions

What is your minimum order quantity for lathe job work?

We accept orders from a single piece upwards. There is no minimum — whether you need 1 emergency spare or 1,000-piece production run, we can help.

How long does CNC lathe job work take?

Most standard jobs are completed within 24–48 hours. Urgent/emergency jobs can be prioritised and sometimes completed same day depending on complexity and queue.

Do you provide material or do I supply it?

Both options are available. You can supply your own raw material or we can source it — MS, SS, brass, CI, bronze, aluminium. Material cost is added to the quote.

What tolerance can you consistently hold?

Our CNC lathes routinely achieve ±0.02 mm on diameter and length. For tighter requirements (h5/H6 fits), we can discuss based on material and drawing.

Do you deliver across Coimbatore and Tamil Nadu?

Yes. We supply to customers across Coimbatore, Ambattur, Guindy, Manali, Irungattukottai, Sriperumbudur and other industrial areas in Tamil Nadu.
