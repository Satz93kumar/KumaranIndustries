Service 01

CNC Facing

CNC facing creates a smooth, flat end surface perpendicular to the part's axis of rotation. It is the starting point for nearly every turned component — ensuring the datum face is square and true before subsequent operations.

We achieve surface roughness values of Ra 0.8–1.6 µm on standard facing passes, and Ra 0.4 µm on fine finishing cuts — suitable for mating flange faces, bearing shoulders, and seal seats.

Surface Finish
Ra 0.4 – 1.6 µm
Flatness
±0.02 mm

Typical Applications

  • Pump casing flange faces
  • Motor end cover mating faces
  • Bearing shoulder preparation
  • Coupling flange faces
  • Valve body seats

Typical Applications

  • Motor rotor shafts & spindles
  • Pump shafts & shaft sleeves
  • Stepped shafts & pins
  • Bearing journal diameters
  • Taper turning & profile turning
Service 02

CNC Turning

CNC turning produces precise cylindrical outside diameters, tapers, and profiles. Our CNC lathes maintain consistent OD tolerances across the full batch — critical for bearing fits, coupling bores, and sliding assemblies.

We perform roughing and finishing passes to achieve the specified tolerance and surface finish, with in-process inspection at every stage.

OD Tolerance
h6 / js6 / k6
Max Diameter
600 mm
Service 03

CNC Boring

CNC boring enlarges and accurately sizes internal diameters in housings, sleeves, and casings. Where drilling leaves a rough, slightly off-centre hole, boring corrects both size and position to tight tolerance.

We specialise in bearing housing bores (H7 fit), pump casing wear ring bores, and mechanical seal chamber bores — where roundness and cylindricity directly affect service life.

ID Tolerance
H7 / H6 / H8
Roundness
≤ 0.01 mm

Typical Applications

  • Motor bearing housing bores
  • Pump wear ring seats
  • Mechanical seal chambers
  • Bush & liner bores
  • Cylinder & sleeve bores

Typical Applications

  • O-ring seal grooves
  • Retaining ring / circlip grooves
  • Oil seal lip recesses
  • Undercut & relief grooves
  • Internal & external grooves
Service 04

CNC Grooving

CNC grooving cuts precise channels, slots, and recesses on cylindrical and internal surfaces. Groove width, depth, and position are critical for sealing performance — and our CNC grooving delivers every time.

We use carbide parting and grooving tools to achieve sharp, burr-free groove edges — essential for O-ring sealing and snap ring retention.

Width Tolerance
±0.02 mm
Depth Tolerance
±0.02 mm
