Our Story

About PrecisionTech CNC

15+ years of precision CNC machining serving Chennai's industrial sector with uncompromising quality and fast turnaround.

Established 2009

Chennai's Trusted Precision Partner

PrecisionTech CNC was founded in 2009 with a single CNC lathe and a commitment to delivering parts that exceed expectations. What started as a small job-work shop in Ambattur has grown into a full-service precision machining facility trusted by over 100 companies across Tamil Nadu.

We specialize in CNC lathe operations — facing, turning, boring, and grooving — and supply precision components to pump manufacturers, motor winding workshops, and general engineering companies.

Our philosophy is simple: right the first time, delivered on time. Every component is inspected before despatch. We never compromise on tolerance.
